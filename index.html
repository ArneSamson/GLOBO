Welcome to our website!

Here, we will post weekly updates of our global classroom journey.

Week 1: introduction

In this week, we got an introduction of the course by Filip. We also got divided into groups. We have Tia and Hailey from the US, Zaida from Mexico and Yannick, Arne, Sidney, Lindsay en Evi from Belgium

Week 2: making a basic website

This week, the students from Belgium thaught the others how to make a basic website. We told them about different HTML tags and how to use them. We also learned how to style our website using CSS.

Week 3: learning about mapping

This week, we learned about mapping, during a lesson by Zaida. We learned how to use different mapping tools and how to use them to visualize data.

Week 4: learning design

This week, we dove into map design, starting with our own stomping grounds. It's been a blast plotting out the nooks and crannies of our hometowns. From favorite hangout spots to hidden gems, we're turning our familiarity into art.

Week 5: a group lesson

This week, we gathered the whole crew, students and teachers alike, for a brainstorming session on our dream futures. We kicked back, shared our wildest aspirations, and let our imaginations run wild. Watching an inspiring video together added fuel to the fire, sparking discussions and igniting passions.

Week 6: final session - what is our view on the future?

This week marked the end of our coil adventure, but it was just the beginning of envisioning our ideal futures. Through stable diffusion, we shared our diverse visions, each painting a picture of what we hoped to see ahead. It was fascinating to see common threads emerge: nature, flying cars, and high-tech marvels. What stood out the most was our collective emphasis on preserving nature, a testament to our commitment to the environment. As we wrapped up, it was clear that while our dreams may vary, our shared values unite us in shaping a brighter, greener tomorrow.
